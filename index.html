Page Management

Select current page:
Pages are just a way to split your story into parts so you can find them and link them easier.
If your story doesn't have any options for the player to choose from, you don't need multiple pages. You can have just one page.
But if you want players to have multiple choices, you should group your messages into different 'pages' so you can link different options to different pages.
